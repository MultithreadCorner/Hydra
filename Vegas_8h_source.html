50 /**
51  * @ingroup numerical_integration
52  * @brief Class to perform numerical integration using Vegas algorithm.
53  *
54  * The VEGAS algorithm of Lepage is based on importance sampling.
55  * It samples points from the probability distribution described by the
56  * function |f|, so that the points are concentrated in the regions that
57  * make the largest contribution to the integral.
58  *
59  * *Find a more complete documentation* [here](https://www.gnu.org/software/gsl/doc/html/montecarlo.html#vegas) .
60  *
61  */
